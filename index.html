Intro

Oh, what an adventure I’ve had! I wish you were there with me to witness it yourself. But no worries, let me recap it briefly!

Raised in the fairly small town of Bhopal, In 2012, I wound up my Senior Year at school with a 78.4% result. After excelling in my entrance exam, I was gratefully accepted to SRM University. Resultingly, the world started appearing a lot more culturally plural. There I learned a ton of coding, made some lifelong friends, received straight A’s in my projects, led teams for the largest student organization in the world - AIESEC, but most importantly graduated as the first engineer in the family!

Employment followed shortly, at a Fortune 500 Company, Cognizant. I organized myself into being an employee, learned the tropes of the corporate industry, impressed my first global clients from Germany, delivering them their primary website. But in the interest of working on more bleeding edge technologies, I left my cushy job at Cognizant, to work for a 10 member educational startup named Peerbuds, a venture by San Francisco based Klouddata Inc. That’s when the adventure really started.

After 10 months of platform development, we were all set to change the world's educational system. Meanwhile, Blockchain became the latest buzz and we decided to ride that wave. This radical shift in firms direction shrunk the team to 3 members, the CEO, CTO and me. Undeterred by the setbacks we rebranded ourselves into The Blockchain University (TBU) and I got promoted as the product lead.

4 months into development, we 3 joined an accelerator, wrote a white paper, built a new blockchain-based protocol and used it to build our platform. It was at this time when I had my first taste of Owning a product, as I had to take up responsibilities in developing product strategy, building a tech community and instructing at blockchain workshops, all the while still leading the platform development. But, like all things good, TBU was nearing its end due to the profitless platform and plateauing growth. In early-2019, sensing TBU's impending doom, I switched to First Walkin Technologies (Walkin) as a Product Lead, hoping for a comparably stable gig, destiny, however, had different plans.

6 months into the job, a major event happened, our Primary investor and biggest client passed away. This sent our firm into a frenzy, but this time I was committed not to let circumstances affect the organization, as now I had a voice at the table. Resultantly, my team was chosen to implement one of India's largest customer loyalty programs, for Dominos and we delivered. As a result, this multi-million dollar contract pulled the firm out of the red. Currently, I lead the team developing our SaaS-based experience management platform, at present our firm's most sought-after product.

Today, I've become adept at building impeccable products at large scale and constantly getting better.

Thanks for reading!
